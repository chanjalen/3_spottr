Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 5, 2026  ·  Last updated: March 5, 2026

Spottr ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Spottr mobile application. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Spottr you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Account information

  • Email address
  • Username and display name
  • Date of birth (used to enforce our 13+ age requirement)
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Password (stored as a one-way hash — we never see your plain-text password)

Profile information

  • Profile photo
  • Bio

Fitness and activity data

  • Workout logs (exercises, sets, reps, weight, duration)
  • Personal records
  • Workout streaks and total workout counts
  • Rest days and workout goals
  • Check-ins at gyms

Social content

  • Posts, check-ins, comments, and reactions you create
  • Friend and follow connections
  • Group memberships

Messages

  • Direct messages and group chat content between users

Media

  • Photos and videos you upload to posts, check-ins, or your profile

Device information

  • Push notification token (to deliver notifications to your device)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To display your profile and content to other users based on your privacy settings
  • To calculate and display workout streaks, personal records, and leaderboards
  • To deliver push notifications you have opted into
  • To enable messaging between users
  • To operate gym check-in and workout invite features
  • To enforce our Terms of Service and age requirements

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your data for advertising.

3. Third-Party Services

Spottr uses the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Google Sign-In — if you choose to sign in with Google, Google processes your authentication. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Expo (Expo Push Notifications) — your push notification token is routed through Expo's notification infrastructure. See Expo's Privacy Policy.

4. Your Privacy Controls

You can control who sees your data from within the app:

  • Profile visibility — Public, Friends Only, or Only Me
  • Workout post visibility — Public, Friends Only, or Only Me
  • Check-in visibility — control separately for friends, following, organizations, and gyms
  • Online status — show or hide whether you are active
  • Personal records — show or hide on your profile
  • Streak — show or hide on your profile
  • Friend requests — allow or disable incoming requests
  • Notifications — granular control over every notification type

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, your profile, posts, check-ins, workouts, messages, and all associated data are permanently deleted from our systems. Some residual data may remain in encrypted backups for up to 30 days before being overwritten.

6. Children's Privacy

Spottr requires users to be at least 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Account Deletion

You can permanently delete your account at any time from within the app: Profile → Edit Profile → Delete Account. This immediately and permanently removes your account and all associated data.

8. Security

Passwords are stored using one-way hashing. Authentication tokens are stored in your device's secure enclave (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore) using encrypted storage. All data is transmitted over HTTPS.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
